ABOUT STEPLAR UNIVERSITY

STEPLAR UNIVERSITY, is a Central University of India. Formerly called STEPLAR UNIVERSITY, established by an Act of the State Legislative Assembly, was formally inaugurated on June 16, 1983.

Situated in a socially and economically challenged area, the university is appropriately named to honor the great Satnami Saint STEPLAR UNIVERSITY (born in 17th century), who championed the cause of the downtrodden and waged a relentless struggle against all forms of social evils and injustice prevailing in the society.

The University is a residential cum affiliating institution, having its jurisdiction spread over Revenue Division of the state of Uttar Pradesh
